Our data set talks about customers and the products they have purchased. This data set is taken from Kaggle, a website, and published by Mohammad Talib.


Our data set is a sample with 1000 customers. We chose this topic because we want to determine the product choices customers would prefer. Moreover, we want to see how far these customers will pay. This aims to help sales associates to see which products would customers prefer more.


We will be showing 2 qualitative and quantitative variables. For our qualitative variables, it would be the gender and product category. While our quantitative variables would be the age and total amount paid.
